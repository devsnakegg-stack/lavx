Advanced, professional-grade Lavalink client for Discord.js.

Music Control

.rewind / .forward(ms)

Jump back or ahead in the current track.

.fadeIn / .fadeOut(ms)

Smoothly transition audio volume.

.bassboost / .nightcore / .vaporwave()

Instantly apply popular audio filters.

.balance / .mono / .stereo()

Fine-grained control over audio channels.

Advanced Queue

.jump(index)

Instantly play a track at a specific position.

.move / .swap(from, to)

Reorganize your queue with ease.

.dedupe()

Clean up duplicate tracks from your queue.

History System

A memory-safe ring buffer for tracking recently played tracks.

history.get(max): Retrieve last tracks.

history.last(): Get the most recent track metadata.

history.size(): Current history count.
